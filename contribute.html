Contribute

Our project is focused on artists and their work, and we're more than happy to get in touch with you if you're interested in contributing.

At the moment this project is still in the launch phase, however if you'd like the get in contact with us the best way would be to reach out to us on Instragram.

Once our network of vending machines is up and running, this page will contain information for any artists interested in contributing, including:

  • - What kind of art we're focused on—minor pieces, not big premium ones
  • - Size constraints so that the work fits in our machines
  • - Details about payment for your work
  • - Locations available
  • - Approximate expected sales numbers, based on previous data

Partnerships & Residencies

We'll also be looking to partner with artists who are interested in becoming more involved with the project. Information about that will be listed here, including:

  • - Filling an entire machine with just your work
  • - Collaborations to design the outside of the machine itself, whether with wraps or something else
  • - Possibly commissioning work specifically for our vending machines
  • - Discussing the nature of art in vending machines, maybe as a new subgenre

Event history

Ideas Worth Vending frequently partners with cultural events around Sydney in order to share art with more people and better connect with the community. From art festivals to zine fairs, these events give customers and artists alike a chance to explore their creativity in a unique setting, and be part of something greater than the sum of its parts.

...this is in theory, of course! We haven't actually hosted or partaken in any events yet, all of the events listed on this page are speculative placeholders.

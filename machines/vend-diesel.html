Vend Diesel

Vend Diesel is a vending machine located at the Garden Lounge Creative Space in Newtown. It was the first vending machine we put together, and served as a proof of concept for our art vending machine network.

It's covered in a collage of images taken from various magazines and books, and is currently filled with works produced by both artists and the project's original team members.

Inside you can find a range of zines, art prints, post cards and bookmarks, each ranging in price from $2 to $8. Vend Diesel is an older model vending machine, so only takes coins, and doesn't provide change.

You can preview the items inside using our online virtual vending machine, but to see what's inside each item you'll need to visit Vend Diesel in person and pick something up yourself!

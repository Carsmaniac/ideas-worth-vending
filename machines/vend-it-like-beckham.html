Vend It Like Beckham

Vend It Like Beckham is an imaginary vending machine located at the Junction Cafe in Haymarket.

Our only vending machine in operation at the moment is Vend Diesel (located at the Garden Lounge in Newtown), so Beckham doesn't exist yet, and we don't have any plans for the Junction Cafe to host one of our machines in the future (but we're not ruling them out!).

If Beckham was a real machine, this page would explain the design of the machine itself, list the items it contains, and provide info about its featured artists.

Our goal is to create a network of vending machines across the city, each filled with art and other thought-provoking items!

You'll eventually be able to preview the items inside using our online virtual vending machine, but for now you can only preview the items inside Vend Diesel.
